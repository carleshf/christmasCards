Geek Christmas Card for 2021-2022

Thsi year I wanted to emulate the old games where you had to move from room to room looking for objects and talking to static characters that had one or two different messages for you. So, also using the port p5js, I wrote a mini-game where you have to collect all the presents, scattered across all the rooms in the office, before completing the game.
